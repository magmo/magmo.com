    Mission

    Magmo is a team of humble R&D engineers friends, willing to take risks developing, germinating, and grooming stretch-goal research ideas with a focus on scaling Ethereum

    Values

    • We:
    • Work on open-source, public goods research projects
    • Indiscriminately aim to improve layer 2 technologies
    • Focus on user pain points, without sacrificing security
    • Invite conversation with anyone, and collaborate

    • We don't:
    • Have egos about credit: our ideas are your ideas
    • Aim to commercialize our output: your success is our success
